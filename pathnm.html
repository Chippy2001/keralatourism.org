Top Destinations

Perinad Lake

Perinad Lake is a beautiful lake located about 10 km from Pathanamthitta town. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, boat cruises, and birdwatching.

Mallapuram Dam

Mallapuram Dam is a dam located on the Pamba River, about 15 km from Pathanamthitta town. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, boating activities, and wildlife.

Ranni Hills

Ranni Hills is a hill station located in the Western Ghats, about 30 km from Pathanamthitta town. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and trekking trails.

Konni Forest Reserve

Konni Forest Reserve is a wildlife reserve located about 20 km from Pathanamthitta town. It is home to a variety of animals, including elephants, tigers, leopards, and deer.

Gavi

Gavi is a remote eco-tourism destination located in the Western Ghats, about 40 km from Pathanamthitta town. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, wildlife, and trekking trails.

Aranmula Snake Boat Race

Aranmula Snake Boat Race is a traditional snake boat race held every year at Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple. It is a popular tourist attraction and a major event in the Kerala calendar.
