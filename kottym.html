Top Destinations

Ilaveezha Poonchira

Ilaveezha Poonchira is a hill station located in the Western Ghats, about 30 km from Kottayam town. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and trekking trails.

Meenachil River

Meenachil River is a river that flows through Kottayam district. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty and boating activities.

Vaikom Mahadeva Temple

Vaikom Mahadeva Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the town of Vaikom. It is one of the most important Shiva temples in Kerala..

Illikkal-Kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a rock formation located in the Moonnilavu village of Meenachil taluk in Kottayam district, Kerala, India. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty and hiking trails.

Thangal Hill

Thangal Hill is a hill located in the town of Kottayam. It is known for its scenic beauty and religious importance. Thangal Hill is a great place to go hiking and to learn about the religious history of Kerala.

Kanjirappally Church

The Kanjirappally Church is a Roman Catholic church located in the town of Kanjirappally. It is one of the largest churches in Kerala. The Kanjirappally Church is known for its beautiful architecture and its annual Kanjirappally Perunal festival.
