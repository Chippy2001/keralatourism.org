Top Destinations

Alleppey Backwaters

The Alleppey Backwaters are a network of canals, lakes, and rivers that crisscross Alappuzha district. They are a popular tourist destination for their scenic beauty and houseboat cruises.

Alappuzha Beach

Alappuzha Beach is a beautiful beach located on the Arabian Sea. It is a popular tourist destination for its swimming, sunbathing, and sunsets.

Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary

Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary is a bird sanctuary located on the Vembanad Lake. It is home to a variety of birds, including herons, egrets, and kingfishers.

Krishnapuram Palace

Krishnapuram Palace is a palace located in the town of Kayamkulam. It is now a museum that houses a collection of artifacts from the Travancore royal family.

Kuttanad

Kuttanad is a region in Alappuzha district that is known for its lush paddy fields and canals. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty and boat cruises..

Mannarasala Sree Nagaraja Temple

Mannarasala Sree Nagaraja Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the serpent god Nagaraja, located in the village of Mannarasala in Alappuzha district, Kerala, India. It is one of the most important temples dedicated to Nagaraja in Kerala..
