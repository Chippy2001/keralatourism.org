Top Destinations

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach is a popular tourist destination in Trivandrum district, known for its beautiful beaches, palm-fringed shores, and backwaters.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

Padmanabhaswamy Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, located in the heart of Trivandrum city. It is one of the richest temples in the world, and its vaults are said to contain billions of dollars worth of treasure.

Ponmudi Hill Station

Ponmudi Hill Station is a hill station located in the Western Ghats, about 61 km from Trivandrum city. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and trekking trails.

Neyyar Dam

Neyyar Dam is a dam located on the Neyyar River, about 30 km from Trivandrum city. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, boating activities, and wildlife.

Agastya Koodam

Agastya Koodam is a mountain peak in the Western Ghats, located about 60 km from Trivandrum city. It is a popular trekking destination for its challenging trails and stunning views.

Trivandrum Zoo

Trivandrum Zoo is a zoo located in the heart of Trivandrum city. It is home to a variety of animals, including elephants, lions, tigers, leopards, and gorillas.
