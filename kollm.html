Top Destinations

Houseboat Cruise

Kollam is known as the "Land of Lakes and Backwaters". Taking a houseboat cruise through the backwaters is a must-do experience when visiting Kollam.

Kollam Beach

Kollam Beach is a popular beach destination in Kollam, known for its beautiful beaches, palm-fringed shores, and sunsets.

Thenmala Eco-Tourism Village

Thenmala Eco-Tourism Village is a sustainable tourism destination located about 60 km from Kollam. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, adventure activities, and eco-friendly accommodations.

Thusharagiri Waterfalls

Thusharagiri Waterfalls is a beautiful waterfall located about 50 km from Kollam. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty and trekking trails.

Ashtamudi Lake

Ashtamudi Lake is the largest lake in Kerala, located in Kollam district. It is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty, boat cruises, and birdwatching.

Jatayu Earth's Center

Jatayu Earth Center, also known as Jatayu Nature Park or Jatayu Rock, is a park and tourism centre at Chadayamangalam in Kollam district of Kerala, India. It stands at an altitude of 350m above the mean sea level.
